Lines that start with a '#' will be ignored
Name of the recipe goes first followed by each ingredient which is followed by the amount
Each element is separated by commas
Max number of ingredients is two
name1,ingredient1,amount1,ingredient2,amount2
For example:
scotch on the rocks,blended scotch,4 oz
vodka martini,unflavored vodka,6 oz,vermouth,1.5 oz
